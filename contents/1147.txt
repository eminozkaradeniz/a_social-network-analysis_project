Click here to learn 2019 DEYOS results Candidates can get “2019 DEYOS Result Document” from Buca Faculty of Education in person. Candidates who have at least 85 points for Medicine Program, at least 80 points for the Law Program, at least 75 points for the Architecture and Engineering Programs, at least 70 points for the Teaching Programs (including the Guidance and Psychological Counseling Program) and for other programs at least 50 points or over from 2019 DEYOS, have the right to make the preferences. Candidates who want to apply the department which accept students with “Talent Test” will not make preferences and apply to relevant Faculty or School in condition to have at least 30 points or over from 2019 DEYOS. Talent Test Calendar and Explanations for 2019-2020 Academic Year The candidates will be placed to the related departments in accordance with their ranking in the exam and within the specified quota. In case candidates have equal scores, the priority for placement will be given to the younger candidate. Beginning of the Process of Preference : 1th July, 2019 End of the Process of Preference             : 5th July, 2019 The preference screen will be active on the link below starting from 1th July, 2019 and will be deactivated after 5th July, 2019 . The Preference Address: http://debis.deu.edu.tr/DEUWeb/Yos Preference Guide NOTICE! Candidates should pay attention to newly determined tuition fees before making a choice. In accordance with the pursuing provisions of the Council of Ministers Decision on tuition fees; candidates who will enroll to the formal education programs those who are Syrian citizens, Expatriation Permit Document holders (Blue ID holder) and candidates who have T.R. nationality those who completed all of secondary education (high school) in a foreign country other than TRNC, tuition fees (during the normal education period of the program) will be paid by Presidency For Turks Abroad And Related Communities.
