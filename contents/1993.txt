Turkish Higher Education Council (YÖK) has recently published the strategies for the time period of 2018-2022. In this seminar, the internationalisation strategies of YÖK are explained. One of the important components of internationalisation is exchange programmes. Erasmus+ exchange programme is also explained in the seminar. In conclusion, internationalisation will be an important part of the academic life in near future.
