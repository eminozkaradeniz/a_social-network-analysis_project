Applicants with international exam score/diploma grade accepted by the Senate of the University will have the right to apply for the additional application. After the applications are completed, the documents submitted by the applicants will be evaluated by the commission and the applications will be placed according to their preferences. Candidates listed in the attached list will apply only for additional placement.
