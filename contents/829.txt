The course/subject titles of the all-degree programmes are easily accessible in the course catalog. Dokuz Eylül University publishes the course catalog in Turkish and in English. All interested parties can easily access the catalog which provides detailed, user-friendly and up-to-date information on the institution’s learning environment involving the qualifications offered, the learning, teaching and assessment procedures, the level of programmes. The course catalog also involves information on the programme outcomes and the contact information for the programme and courses. The course catalogue giving detailed information about the degree programmes is available at the homepage of the university and you can reach it here:
