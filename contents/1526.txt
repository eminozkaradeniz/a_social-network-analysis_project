After its foundation, a total of 21 graduate programs with 322 students were transferred to the Institute of Educational Sciences, 16 from the Institute of Social Sciences and 5 from the Institute of Science. Recently, 51 undergraduate programs, 11 departments, 846 masters and doctoral students have been continuing education and training activities.
