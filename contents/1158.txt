Student number is in the “Placement Document” IMPORTANT! In accordance with the relevant regulation and the decision of the Council of Higher Education Executive Board, it is not possible to enroll for programs at the same level (more than one associate’s degree or more than one bachelor’s degree program) except for open education and distance education programs. In this respect, students who prefer to enroll in our university are required to delete their registration at previous higher education institutions (except open and distance education programs). IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT ABOUT HEALTH REPORTS In accordance with the decision taken at the Higher Education Executive Board’s meeting dated 28.08.2020; Candidates for whom a medical report is required to enroll in the program they have been placed in will be able to enroll with a single physician report issued to show that they do not have any illnesses. Single physician reports will be accepted instead of medical reports. Approved health report from abroad is also accepted, but the health report must be uploaded with its Turkish translation.
